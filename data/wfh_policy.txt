========================================================================
GLOBAL MNC INC. - WORK FROM HOME (WFH) & HYBRID WORK POLICY
========================================================================

1. OBJECTIVE
This policy defines the structural guidelines, operational expectations, and reimbursement structures for employees working remotely or operating under a hybrid workplace model.

2. WORKPLACE MODELS

2.1 Hybrid Model (Standard Operating Procedure)
- All technical and non-technical staff members are required to work from their designated base office location for a minimum of 3 days per week (typically Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday).
- Mondays and Fridays are designated as flexible remote working days, subject to project deliverables and client requirements.

2.2 Full Remote / Work From Home (WFH)
- Full WFH status is granted only under exceptional medical or operational circumstances and requires written sign-off from the Department Head (VP level) and the HR Business Partner (HRBP).
- Employees operating on approved full WFH are required to maintain core working hours (10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time) for synchronous team collaboration.

3. INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY & SECURITY PROTOCOLS

3.1 Device & Network Compliance
- Production Code Deployment: Under no circumstances shall personal computing devices, laptops, or tablets be used to deploy code to production environments or access client environments. All development work must occur on company-issued assets.
- VPN Mandate: Access to corporate networks, internal repositories, and code bases from public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks is strictly prohibited. Employees must connect via the official corporate Virtual Private Network (VPN) with multi-factor authentication (MFA) enabled.

4. EXPENSE ALLOWANCES & ALLOWED STIPENDS

4.1 One-time Remote Setup Allowance
- Active Software Engineers and Senior Developers are eligible for a one-time remote workspace setup reimbursement of up to $500 (or equivalent regional currency). 
- Eligible items for reimbursement include ergonomic office chairs, external monitors, keyboards, mice, and UPS backup units. Receipts must be submitted via the expense portal within 60 days of purchase.

4.2 Monthly Internet & Communication Stipend
- All employees working in hybrid or full remote modes are eligible for a monthly internet connectivity stipend of $30 to ensure high-speed broadband availability (minimum required speed: 50 Mbps).

5. DATA PRIVACY & CONFIDENTIALITY
Employees must ensure that company-related audio/video calls are conducted in private spaces where screen contents and conversations remain confidential and protected from non-employees.
