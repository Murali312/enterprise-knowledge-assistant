========================================================================
GLOBAL MNC INC. - ENTERPRISE HUMAN RESOURCES LEAVE POLICY (v2026.1)
========================================================================

1. PURPOSE & SCOPE
This document outlines the leave entitlements and attendance regulations for all full-time permanent employees of Global MNC Inc. All leaves are calculated on a calendar year basis (January 1st to December 31st).

2. CATEGORIES OF LEAVE

2.1 Privileged Leave (PL) / Earned Leave (EL)
- Entitlement: All full-time employees accrue 1.75 days of Privileged Leave for every completed month of service, totaling 21 days per calendar year.
- Availing Leave: A prior notice of at least 7 business days is mandatory for applying for more than 3 consecutive days of PL. Approval must be obtained from the immediate Reporting Manager via the HR Portal.
- Carry Forward: A maximum of 5 unused Privileged Leaves can be carried forward to the next calendar year. Any additional accrued, unused PLs beyond this threshold will automatically lapse on December 31st.
- Encashment: Leave encashment is permitted only at the time of separation (resignation or retirement) based on the employee's basic salary component.

2.2 Casual & Sick Leave (CSL)
- Entitlement: Employees are credited with 12 days of Casual & Sick Leave at the beginning of the calendar year (pro-rated for mid-year joiners).
- Documentation: Any sick leave extending beyond 3 consecutive business days requires a valid medical certificate issued by a registered medical practitioner to be uploaded to the HR portal within 48 hours of resuming work.
- Restrictions: CSL cannot be combined with Privileged Leave (PL). Unused CSL cannot be carried forward to the next calendar year and cannot be encashed.

2.3 Parental Leave
- Maternity Leave: Eligible female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of fully paid maternity leave as per statutory mandates. A minimum of 80 days of continuous employment prior to the expected delivery date is required.
- Paternity Leave: Eligible male employees are entitled to 5 business days of paid paternity leave, which must be utilized within 6 months of the child's birth.

3. HOLIDAYS
The company observes a total of 10 mandatory public holidays per year, varying by regional office location. The annual holiday list is published on the company intranet on or before December 15th of the preceding year.
